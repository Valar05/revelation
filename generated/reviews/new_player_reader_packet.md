# New Player Reader Packet

## Role

You are a first-time player reading these playable scenario beats with minimal context. Do not assume design notes, source texts, or hidden lore. Judge only what the game text gives you.

## Minimal Context

- You are reading a text-first symbolic horror roguelike called Revelation.
- The player issues commands for Torah, an Institute field lead, and a small containment squad.
- Each mission presents a concrete anomaly, a tactical choice, and a consequence.
- The game should feel procedural, readable, and tense without needing external scripture/source knowledge.

## Review Questions

For each mission, answer briefly:
1. What is happening right now?
2. What is the immediate danger or clock?
3. Who is present, who is remote, and what does each named person appear able to do?
4. What are the choices asking you to decide?
5. Do the choice outcomes branch in a meaningful way, or do they feel like the same resolution with different names?
6. Are any events, causes, or resolutions presented out of chronological order?
7. What information did you need but did not get?

## Output Format

Return:
- A one-paragraph overall comprehension verdict.
- A table with one row per mission: `mission`, `clear?`, `chronology`, `branching`, `missing context`, `most confusing line`.
- A short list of the three highest-impact fixes.
